Chocolate Banana Smoothie

Serves: 2
Active Time: 5 minutes | Total Time: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 100g oats
  • 100g beans
  • 30g nuts
  • 1 heaped tbsp cocoa
  • 1/4 tsp coffee beans (optional)
  • 2 frozen bananas
  • 1 cup water

Directions

  1. Add all ingredients to blender in listed order
  2. Blend until smooth

Notes

  • Bananas are best cut and frozen at least one week before use
  • Beans can be from dried or canned, canned beans should be low sodium and rinsed
  • Beans can be black, white or pinto, but chickpeas are not suggested
