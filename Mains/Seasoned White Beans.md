## 🛒 Ingredients
- **1 cup** dried white beans, such as cannellini or great northern
- **½ cup** sliced onion
- **2-3 cloves** garlic, smashed with the side of a knife
- **2-3 sprigs** fresh herbs, such as rosemary, thyme, marjoram or sage
- **1** bay leaf
- **1-2** dried chile de arbol, or 1 teaspoon crushed red chili
- Salt

## 👩‍🍳 Directions
1. **Soak the beans** overnight.
2. Put the **beans, onion, garlic, herb sprigs, bay leaf and chili** in a pressure cooker insert. 
3. Add enough **water to cover the beans** by 2-3 inches. 
4. Lock the lid in place and **cook 20-25 minutes at high pressure**. Allow the steam to release naturally.
5. Add **salt** to taste.
