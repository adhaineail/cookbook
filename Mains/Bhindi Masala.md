## 🛒 Ingredients
- **250 g** okra pieces (or other vegetables like potatoes)
- **5 tablespons** oil
- **1 teaspoon** cumin seeds
- **½ teaspoon** mustard seeds
- **1 cup** chopped onions (**2** onions)
- **1 cup** tomato puree (**2** tomatoes)
- **½ teaspoon** ginger, minced
- **½ teaspoon** garlic, minced
- **1 sprig** curry leaves
- **1 teaspoon** roasted cumin powder
- **1 teaspoon** coriander powder
- **½ teaspoon** garam masala
- **2 pinches** turmeric
- **1 teaspoon** red chilli powder
- **½ teaspoon** salt

## 👩‍🍳 Directions
1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil and **fry okra pieces** and fry till they turn dark. Set aside.
2. Heat 2 tablespoons of oil and **roast cumin and mustard seeds**.
3. Add thinly chopped **onions** and fry till they turn light golden. While the onions are changing colour, add **curry leaves**.
4. When onions change colour, add **minced ginger and garlic**, **salt**, and **remaining spices**, then fry till oil separates.
5. Add **tomato paste** and cook until oil separates.
6. After the oil rises to the surface, add the **okra pieces** and let it cook for 3-4 minutes. Remove from fire when oil separates.
