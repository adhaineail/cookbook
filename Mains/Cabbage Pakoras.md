## 🛒 Ingredients
- **1 cup** cabbage finely shredded
- **½ onion** finely sliced
- **1** green chilli finely chopped
- **1 tablespoon** cilantro finely chopped
- **1 teaspoon** minced garlic ginger or ginger garlic paste
- **½ teaspoon** garam masala
- **½ teaspoon** red chilli powder or paprika
- **¼ teaspoon** turmeric powder
- **¼ teaspoon** ajwain or carrom seeds or use dry oregano
- **¾ cup** chickpea flour or besan
- **2 tablespoons** rice flour or cornstarch (optional)
- **½ teaspoon** salt or to taste
- **1 teaspoon** olive oil
- Oil for frying
- Some water

## 👩‍🍳 Directions
1. Mix **all ingredients but flours and oil** in a large bowl and let rest for 5 minutes.
2. **Add flours and oil**, and mix well with hands. 
3. Add very little **water** to form the batter. It should not be too runny especially for the air fried version.

### Air Fryer
1. **Preheat air fryer** to 360°F or 180°C for 4 minutes. Put a perforated parchment paper to line air fryer basket.
2. Now **form small pakoras** from the batter and place on air fryer basket, with space between them.
3. **Air fry at for 5 minutes**. Brush pakoras with oil and flip them. Air fry for another 3-5 minutes.
4. If needed, increase the temperature to 200°C or 390°F and air fry for 2-3 minutes more till they become browned and well cooked inside.

### Baking
1. **Preheat oven** to 350°F or 175°C. Line a baking tray with parchment paper and dollop pakoras onto it.
2. **Bake for 10 minutes**. 
3. Flip them and bake for 5-7 minutes more or until they have crispy edges.

### Pan fry
1. **Heat pan** to medium-hot and add oil.
2. Dollop on small pakoras and **fry on each side** for about 3 minutes, or until brown.

## 📝 Notes
- Keep pakoras small and not too runny
- Can use red and/or green cabbage
- Finely shred vegetables
- Can use cornstarch instead of rice flour, or sub with besan, will be less crispy though
