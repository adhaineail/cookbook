Thai Curry

Serves: 8
Active Time: 45 minutes
Total Time: 45 minutes

Ingredients:
- 2 cans curry paste
- 1 onion, sliced
- 8 makrut lime leaves
- 1 can coconut milk
- 2 cups water
- 6 dried dates
- 1/2 cup cashews
- 800g firm tofu, cubed

Directions:
1. Saut√© onions in large pot until translucent.
2. Add curry paste and cook for 3 minutes.
3. Combine cashews, dates, coconut milk, 4 makrut lime leaves, and 2 cups of water to blender and blend for 1 minute.
4. Add blended ingredients to pot with remaining makrut lime leaves and tofu.
5. Simmer for 10 minutes before serving.

Notes:
- Serve with rice and steamed vegetables
- Can add additional aromatics with onions: capsicum, mushrooms, etc.
- For satay variation, substitute 1 can curry paste for 2 tbps curry powder and 1/2 cup peanut butter.
