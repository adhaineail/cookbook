Best Greens

Serves: 4
Active Time: 20min
Total Time: 30min

Ingredients:
- .5 medium onion, diced
- 1 jalapeno, diced
- 3 garlic cloves, minced
- large pinch salt
- 1 bunch kale, or collards, or other greens, chopped
- 1 Tbsp water
- 1 tsp ground cumin
- .5 tsp ground coriander
- 2 tsp smoked paprika

Directions:
1. Saute onions and jalapeno for 3-5min, then add garlic and salt and saute for another min.
2. Add greens and water and stir, saute for a few min.
3. Add spices, mix well, saute for another min until greens have wilted and darkened, but before they go soft or too dark.
