Sweet Potato Tortillas

Serves: 16
Active Time: 30 min
Total Time: 45 min

Ingredients:
- 4 medium sweet potatoes
- 4 cups whole wheat flour
- pinch of salt
- white flour as needed for rolling

Directions:
1. Steam sweet potatoes for 10 min or until fork tender.
2. Mash well.
4. Mix in the flour and salt.
5. Divide dough into 16 balls. Dust surface and roller liberally with white flour and roll out each ball to size of pan, about 10 inches.
6. Cook on med-hot pan for 1-2min each side until brown spots form. Careful not to burn!

Notes:
- Dough can be made ahead of time, and tortillas freeze very well.
