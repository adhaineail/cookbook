## 🛒 Ingredients
- **4** medium sweet potatoes
- **4 cups** whole wheat flour
- **1-1 ½ teaspoons** salt
- White flour as needed for rolling

## 👩‍🍳 Directions
1. Steam **sweet potatoes** for 10 minutes or until fork tender.
2. Mash well or blend in food processor until pureed.
3. Mix in the **flour** and **salt**. Add more flour if it is too sticky.
4. **Divide dough** into 16 balls. Dust surface and roller liberally with white flour and roll out each ball to size of pan, about 10 inches.
5. Cook on medium-hot pan for 1-2 minutes each side until brown spots form. Careful not to burn!

## 📝 Notes
- Dough can be made ahead of time, and tortillas freeze very well.
