French Silk Pie

Serves: 12
Active Time: 1 hour
Total Time: 7 hours

Ingredients:
- 4 Eggs
- 1 cup Granulated Sugar
- 250g Dark Chocolate
- 2 tbsp Cocoa
- 1/4 tsp Coffee
- 2 tsp Vanilla
- 1 cup Butter
- 1 cup Cream
- 4 tsp Powdered Sugar
- 1 cup Cream, for topping
- 1 tsp Vanilla, for topping
- 1/2 cup Powdered Sugar, for topping

Directions:
1. Whisk eggs with granulated sugar and heat to 70c until mixture coats back of spoon.
2. Stir in chocolate, cocoa, coffee, and vanilla, then cool until warm.
3. Beat butter until light and fluffy, 2 to 3 minutes.
4. Add in chocolate mixture and beat on high until light and fluffy, 5 minutes.
5. Beat cream until thick, then add powdered sugar and beat until stiff peaks form.
6. Combine the two mixtures, then pour into crust and chill for 6 hours.
7. Beat all ingredients for topping on high until stiff peaks form, then add to pie.
