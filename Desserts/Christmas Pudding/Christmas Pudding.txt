Christmas Pudding

Serves: 16
Active Time: 1 hour
Total Time: 1 week

Ingredients:
- 250g currants
- 250g sultanas
- 500g raisins
- 1/4 cup brandy
- 250g butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 5 eggs, 1 at a time
- 1 cup flour, divided between fruit and batter
- 1 tsp soda
- 1 tsp mixed spice (equal cinnamon, nutmeg and ginger)
- 2 cups breadcrumbs, from stale white bread

Directions:
1. Soak dried fruit with brandy overnight.
2. Beat butter with sugar, adding eggs one at a time.
3. Add dry ingredients and soaked fruit to batter and mix.
4. Dampen cloth in hot water, then put in bowl and coat with flour.
5. Add pudding mixture onto cloth and string up.
6. Boil pudding for 3 hours, submerged and covered.
7. Hang until dried.
8. Boil pudding again for 1 hour.
