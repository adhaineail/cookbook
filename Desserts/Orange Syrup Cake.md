## 🛒 Ingredients
- **2** oranges
- **6** eggs
- **250 g** caster sugar
- **250 g** almond meal
- **1 heaped teaspoon** baking powder

## 👩‍🍳 Directions
1. **Boil oranges** whole for 2 hours.
2. Place **sugar** and **eggs** in food processor and mix until lighter in colour.
3. Chop **oranges** and place in food processor and mix for 30 seconds.
4. Add **almond meal** and **baking powder** and mix for 20 seconds.
5. Bake in 8" cake tin (7" for taller cake) at 160°C in a fan forced oven for 45 minutes. Adjust times accordingly and do the skewer test.
