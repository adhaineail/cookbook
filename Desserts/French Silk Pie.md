## 🛒 Ingredients
- ¼ eggs
- **1 cup** granulated sugar
- **250 g** dark chocolate
- **2 tablespoons** cocoa
- **¼ teaspoon** coffee
- **2 teaspoons** vanilla
- **1 cup** butter
- **1 cup** cream
- **4 teaspoons** powdered sugar
- **1 cup** cream, for topping
- **1 teaspoon** vanilla, for topping
- **½ cup** powdered sugar, for topping

## 👩‍🍳 Directions
1. Whisk **eggs** with **granulated sugar** and heat to 70°C or until mixture coats back of spoon.
2. Stir in **chocolate**, **cocoa**, **coffee**, and **vanilla**, then cool until warm.
3. Beat **butter** until light and fluffy, 2 to 3 minutes.
4. Add in **chocolate mixture** and beat on high until light and fluffy, 5 minutes.
5. Beat **cream** until thick, then add **powdered sugar** and beat until stiff peaks form.
6. Combine the two mixtures, then pour into crust and **chill for 6 hours**.
7. Beat all topping ingredients on high until stiff peaks form, then add to pie.
