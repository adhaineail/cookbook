Bok Choy with Oyster Sauce

Serves: 2
Active Time: 15 min
Total Time: 30 min

Ingredients:
- 1 tsp oil
- 3 garlic cloves, sliced thinly
- 1 Thumb of ginger, minced
- 2 medium bok choy
- 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
- 1 Tbsp veg oyster sauce

Directions:
1. Prep bok choy by pulling off all outer leaves and soaking in water.
2. Pull or cut off thick stems, cut as needed.
3. Heat oil in pan and saute garlic and ginger for 30 seconds.
4. Saute bok choy stems, cover and steam for 3 minutes. Add water as needed.
5. Add bok choy leaves, stir and 
6. Add sesame oil and oyster sauce.
