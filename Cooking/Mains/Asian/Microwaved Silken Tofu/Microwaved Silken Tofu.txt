Microwaved Silken Tofu

Serves: 2
Active Time: 10 min
Total Time: 15 min

Ingredients:
- 1 block silken tofu
- 1 thumb ginger, minced
- 1 medium green scallion, cut into 1 cm pieces.
- .5 tsp salt
- .5 garlic granules
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp EVOO
- 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
- 2 tsp veg oyster sauce

Directions:
1. Place tofu into a microwave safe container and slice into 5 rectangles.
2. Sprinkle salt, sugar, garlic, ginger, and scallions on top.
4. Pour on soy sauce, EVOO, toasted sesame oil and veg oyster sauce.
5. Cover with a plate and microwave for 4 minutes.
