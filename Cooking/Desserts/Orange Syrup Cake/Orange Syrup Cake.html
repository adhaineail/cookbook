Orange Syrup Cake

Serves: 16
Active Time: 20 minutes | Total Time: 3 hours

Ingredients

  • 2 oranges, cooked for 2 hours
  • 6 eggs
  • 250g caster sugar
  • 250g almond meal
  • 1 heaped tsp baking powder

Directions

  1. Place sugar and eggs in food processor and mix until lighter in colour.
  2. Chop oranges and place in food processor and mix for another 30s.
  3. Add almond meal and baking powder and mix for another 20s.
  4. Bake in 8" cake tin (7" for taller cake) at 160 degrees in a fan forced oven for 45min. Adjust times accordingly and do the skewer test.
